About the company

The business was set up in 1983 by David Drury as sole proprietor. Initially large contracts were set up with local furniture and white goods distributors before branching out into household removals both locally and around Europe.

We have been instrumental in the setting up of large exhibitions displaying large post room equipment in The Excel Center, NEC and Scottish exhibition centres. We can offer the whole bespoke service from planning, setting up the exhibition and breaking down the exhibition, liaising with exhibition centre staff and exhibiting personnel.
